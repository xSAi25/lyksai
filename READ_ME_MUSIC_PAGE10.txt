Background music setup:
- Put your background song as music.mp3 in this same folder.
- It loops until Page 10.
- When Page 10 opens, music fades out.
- If final-song.mp3 exists, the final song tries to play after fade out.

Important browser rule:
Chrome/Edge may block true autoplay with sound on local HTML files until the first tap/click.
This file attempts autoplay immediately and retries on first interaction.
