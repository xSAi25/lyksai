My Message Behind This Song

Hi Mahal ❤️,

This song reminds me of how my life changed when you came into it. Before, there were times na parang hindi ko alam kung saan ako papunta, but somehow everything started to make more sense when I met you.

Hindi man tayo perfect, at hindi rin laging madali ang lahat, pero ikaw ang isa sa mga dahilan kung bakit mas naging masaya, makulay, at meaningful ang bawat araw ko. Sa bawat tawanan, kwentuhan, at simpleng moments natin, mas lalo kong nare-realize kung gaano ako kaswerte na magkaroon ng taong katulad mo.

This song is my way of saying thank you. Thank you for your love, your patience, your understanding, and for choosing me every day. Kahit isang buwan pa lang ang lumipas, you've already given me memories and happiness that I will always treasure.

Happy 1st Monthsary, Mahal. ❤️

No matter where life takes us, I will always choose you.

I love you. ❤️
